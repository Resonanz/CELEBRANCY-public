About Celebrancy for Life

Over the past 35 years, I have had the amazing privilege and honour of being involved with people at significant times in their lives. I have helped people celebrate and mourn in a way that is unique and meaningful and planned by their family. In doing so, I have developed the compassion needed to help people face the challenges of life and walk the journey.
